About the Company

SP Teslenko O.S. is a reliable partner in the food industry with over 20 years of successful experience. We specialize in the production of modern equipment for meat processing, dairy, and canning enterprises, as well as in the high-quality restoration of machinery, enabling businesses to maintain high productivity and efficiency.

Our products are used in enterprises across Ukraine, Poland, Germany, the Czech Republic, Romania, Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, and other countries. Thanks to our equipment, food products for millions of consumers are produced daily on the most modern production lines.

Our Solutions for the Food Industry

We manufacture equipment that meets the most up-to-date requirements and standards. Our key areas of activity include the production of seaming machines, filling lines, conveyors, can-washing machines, and other equipment for the canning industry. Additionally, we offer comprehensive services for installation, modernization, and warranty maintenance.

Our team of experts ensures a personalized approach to each customer, adapting the equipment to production needs. By using frequency converters from leading global manufacturers such as Omron (Japan) and Schneider Electric (Austria), we guarantee smooth and stable operation with the ability to adjust productivity.

Products and Services

Our product range includes:

  • Seaming machines KZK of various modifications for glass and tin cans
  • Canning production lines: from container preparation to sterilization of cans
  • Conveyors for transporting empty and filled cans
  • Machines for filling liquid and paste-like products
  • Labeling and transporting equipment
  • Components and spare parts for the canning industry
  • Machines and Automates for Meat and Meat Products Packaging

We also offer services for the complete supply of production lines, installation, and further equipment maintenance. Our goal is to help customers achieve the best results in production.

Innovations and Customer Adaptation

We are constantly improving our equipment, considering modern trends and market needs. At the customer’s request, we adapt machines for working with different container sizes. Additionally, we can install a steam injection system under the lid on seaming machines, significantly improving the quality of canned meat products.

Our Mission

We aim to create high-quality equipment that helps food manufacturers not only ensure an uninterrupted production process but also improve the quality of the finished products. Our clients are our partners, and together we achieve success.

Global Deliveries

We are proud that our equipment is in demand not only in Ukraine but also abroad. From the moment an order is received to delivery, the lead time ranges from 30 to 120 days, depending on the complexity of the equipment. We guarantee timely delivery and the possibility of early shipments if necessary.
